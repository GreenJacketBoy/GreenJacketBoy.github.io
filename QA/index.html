Q :  The Website is in english but your portfolio is all written in french, why is that ?

A :  Originally, this was just my portfolio Website, so everything was in French, as my portfolio would be mostly visited by french speaking people. I then had the idea to add more than just my portfolio to this Website, mainly because I wanted a place to write articles about whatever came to my mind, and unlike my portfolio, the target audience for them is not limited to french speakers. That and I find it easier to express my thoughts in English.
